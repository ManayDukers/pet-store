Civil War

Escalations rise as a new government order requires superheroes to unmask or retire. Tensions rise even higher when the superhero community is split in half as one half led by Iron Man (Tony Stark) supports government control, while the other half led by Captain America (Steve Rogers) supports superhero freedom.

The Death of Captain America

After the Civil War, Captain America is taken into custody and will be arrested for resisting the government. While at his trial by S.H.I.E.L.D, he is shot by orders of Sin, also known as the Red Skull. Mourning his loss, Iron Man (Tony Stark) and Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff) hunt down the person who killed their friend, as Falcon (Sam Wilson) and The Winter Soldier (Bucky Barnes) hunt down Tony in an attempt for revenge.

Captain America: The First Avenger

After a scientifical experiment transforms him into a superhero capable of extraordinary capabilities, Steve Rogers becomes the number one propaganda America has to offer; Captain America! As the war progresses, Hydra, the secret military organization, begins to unlock the full power of the Tesseract, an item of highly lethal capabilities. Led by the Red Skull, Captain America must go into battle to stop him and Arnim Zola before they take over the world. Through the battle friends will be lost, risks will be taken, and in the end the ultimate sacrifice must be made. Starring Chris Evans, Sebastion Stan, Hugo Weaving, and Hayley Atwell.

World War Hulk

Furious at his "friends" for killing the people he loved, The Hulk (Bruce Banner) returns to earth to wage war on those four who sent him there, The Illuminati, consisting of Iron Man (Tony Stark), Black Bolt (Blackagar Boltagon), Doctor Strange (Stephen Strange), and Mr. Fantastic (Reed Richards). He lays waste to Manhattan, and forces The Illuminati to fight each other. As the war rages, The Sentry (Robert Reynolds) battles with his fear of burning up Manhatten with his powers, but as time runs out, he is faced with a difficult decision: fight The Hulk, or let him destroy the world he loves?
